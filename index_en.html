Profile

Daisuke Taniguchi

I'm working as a JSPS fellow at National Astronomical Observatory of Japan (NAOJ). I'm mainly working on the chemo-dynamics of the Milky Way using (near-infrared) high-resolution spectroscopy of late-type stars. I'm also interested in the development of data-analysis methods and the stellar atmosphere.
